Leopold Aschenbrenner (former OpenAI Superalignment team member) lays out a rigorous analysis of the exponential curves driving frontier AI models.

### Key Insights
* **Compute Expansion:** Unpacking the leap from 10k GPU clusters to million-GPU clusters by 2028.
* **Algorithmic Efficiency:** Algorithmic improvements alone contribute as much efficiency gain per year as compute hardware scaling.
* **National Security & Security:** Why securing model weights and AI infrastructure will become the defining geopolitical priority of the late 2020s.

Whether or not you agree with every timeline projection, this essay provides one of the clearest frameworks for understanding AI scaling trajectories.
